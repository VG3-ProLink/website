05

Who we are

VG3 ProLink combines experience in recruiting, workforce administration, payroll management, business compliance and cross-border mobility support to help organizations deploy technical talent efficiently across the United States.

VG3 is structured as a workforce deployment partner, not a traditional recruiter. The company coordinates the operational layer required to move, employ and support technical personnel during project-based assignments.

Immigration-related matters are coordinated through specialized migration partners. VG3 focuses on workforce deployment, employment administration, mobility and ongoing support.
